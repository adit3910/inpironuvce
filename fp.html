Event Name

Fractured Pixels

Team Size: 1-3

Mode: Offline

Ticket Price: ₹40/person

Cash Prize: Upto ₹4000

Round 1

In the first round, participants have to solve a crossword given to them. Crossword contains 10 technical words for which 10 clues are given. Set of blocks in the crosswords are highlighted . On combining these letters, you get the final answer to the crossword.


Round 2

“8” teams from 1st round will be shortlisted to the 2nd round. In this round participants have to replicate the website projected to them. The website will be created by the organizers using HTML and CSS. The website will be displayed for a duration of 15 mins. Teams have to observe the website in this time period and replicate it. Teams can note down details of the website displayed to them on sheets provided to them.
