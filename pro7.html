About

INSPIRON is an Annual intercollegiate Techno-Managerial fest organised by the Training and Placement Office (TPO), UVCE. The motive is to enable students to express their originality and creativity both as a skill and as an art in a miniature stage. Having a history of more than a decade, this technical extravaganza has been seeing a footfall of over 1500 students every year, who bring in their innovative ideas as a part of events and competitions and reach great heights. The student cliques gear up for campaigning, organising, and decorating giving the fest the glory of a fair.

INSPIRON this year is set to explore and understand the captivating energy, glorious power, and enchanting world of PLANET-B. It’s a totally different and special experience to work on and enlighten knowledge. Living on earth is pleasant but facing the challenges when you are on another planet for the first time is an adventure. The main objective of PLANET-B is to create solutions for a sustainable living in these new lands for better living.

Events

The events of INSPIRON22.O are focused to improve students personal and technical skills and to give them an opportunity that focuses more on the overall technical development of the student. There are events which would also help the participants to play and have fun with their peers. Conflict cannot survive without participation. So, every events vision is to create things and dig deep inside to the core, thus you can find solutions to the surface. “Opportunities are not given but taken. To learn things is not easy but not impossible. So the events conducted on this INSPIRON are going to open the doors for your abilities to acknowledge, learn, present and develop thoroughly.
