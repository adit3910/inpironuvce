Event Name

Hash_it_out

Team Size: 1-4

Mode: Online

Ticket Price: ₹60/person

Cash Prize: Upto ₹4000

Round 1: Web scrapping

2 to 3 websites will be given to the participants 2 days before the main event. Participants have to scrape the website and find the hidden links which consist of tasks and problem statements for the second round. Participants have to submit the code along with the links they have scraped.
Top teams who have submitted correctly and fast will advance to the second round


Round 2: mini hackathon

Based on the links scraped from the first round which consists of tasks and problem statements, participants will build a website on it. The problem statement in the links will be revealed one day before the presentation of the website. Until then the tasks will be hidden in the links. Based on participation number, top 10 teams can be shortlisted and can be advanced to the judges. The tasks will be focused more on backend development. The evaluation criteria also will be majorly based on the backend development.
