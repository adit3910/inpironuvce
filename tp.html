Event Name

Trace space

Team size: 1-3

Mode: Offline

Ticket Price: ₹40/person

Cash Prize: Upto ₹4000

Round 1: SPICEy Circuits

Participants will have to troubleshoot circuits on LTspice to obtain the desired output. 10 faulty circuit files along with the expected waveforms will be given and each circuit will have points allotted to it based on its level of difficulty. The software to be used is LT spice. The expected waveforms can be obtained using transient analysis, AC analysis or DC operating point. Participants will be given 40 minutes to debug and obtain the desired signal and each team can utilize only one computer.


Round 2: Saucy Signals

Participants will have to produce waveforms using MATLAB. The team which accumulates the number of points wins. 10 signals of varying difficulty will be given and each signal will have points allotted to it based on its level of difficulty. The expected signals can be generated using a sum of sines and cosines. The software to be used is MATLAB. The participants will be given 40 mins to generate the given signals and each team can utilize only one computer.
Bonus: Participants get to increase their points by choosing their area of strength : LT Spice Or MATLAB. This should be decided prior to the start of the event. A multiplier of 1.5x will be given
