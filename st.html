Event Name

Shark Tank

Team Size: 1-4

Mode: hybrid

Ticket Price: ₹50/person

Cash Prize: Upto ₹4000

Round 1: Social promo

It is an online round where participants are supposed to pitch their ideas in form of reel, poster or video on Instagram with #InspironSharkTank in the caption / Instagram story. The post should be shared on the Instagram page of the team member's account. The team can campaign in a professional manner. 24 hours time is given for campaigning. The team with most likes on their post qualifies for the next round.


Round 2: Ameliorate

The qualified participants now pitch a defective product which doesn't fulfil its purpose (like a pen that can't write or a knife that can't cut) to the sharks and investments (in crores) are made based on the pitch. 2 minutes of time is given for the pitch. The sharks will now ask questions to challenge the credibility of the product and finally present their investment. The teams are allowed to bargain with the sharks with suitable propositions. 5 minutes time is given for the interaction.


Round 3: Antithetical

In this surprize round each team is tasked

to critisize the other team about their products and at the end make solid points as comeback.
